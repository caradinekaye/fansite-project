Upcoming Projects & Scheduled Guesting

  1. 25 October 2015: The Children's Monologues Production at the Royal Court Theatre

    Danny Boyle, the Slumdog Millionaire filmmaker, will direct the show comprising a sequence of monologues adapted by writers such as Sir David Hare, Neil LaBute and James Graham, retelling the first-hand accounts of 15 young children growing up in the rural township of Rammulotsi, South Africa. Kit Harington alongside James McAvoy, Chiwetel Ejiofor and Christopher Eccleston will star the show.

  2. 08 November 2015: 'Game of Thrones' Press Conference at the Four Seasons Hotel in Beverly Hills, California

  3. 23 November 2015: Guest at The Tonight Show starring Jimmy Fallon

  4. 19 December 2015: Guest at The Ellen DeGeneres Show

  5. 2016 (Final Release Date TBA): 'Brimstone' directed by Martin Koolhoven (Filming)

    Kit Harington, Dakota Fanning and Guy Pearce are set to co-star in Martin Koolhoven's 'Brimstone'. The film follows a young 19th century woman named Liz (Fanning) on the run from a diabolical preacher (Pearce), with (Harington) playing the outlaw role of Samuel.
